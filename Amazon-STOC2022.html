Amazon is organizing a QnA event for STOC participants on Tuesday the 21st June, from 6:45 to 7:45 pm Rome time. Amazon has various programs that allow academics to work on large-scale technical challenges, while they continue to teach and conduct research at their universities. Amazon offers a similar opportunity to PhD students via internships, which could then lead to them working at Amazon. Amazon also grants Research Awards to fund research in areas that support our mission to advance customer-obsessed science. Many members of the theory community have found fruitful careers at Amazon. This event gives you the chance to have Amazon employees and Amazon scholars answer your questions about any of these.

Please send your questions to stoc22-qna@amazon.com. (Let us know if you would like to remain anonymous and we will not reveal who the question is from.)
